Pandit Deendayal Energy University (PDEU)

Pandit Deendayal Energy University (PDEU), located in Gandhinagar, Gujarat, India, is a premier educational institution dedicated to providing quality education in the fields of engineering, technology, and management. Established in 2007, PDEU is recognized for its focus on sustainable energy, innovation, and research, making it a key player in the development of India's energy sector. The university offers undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs in various disciplines, including petroleum engineering, civil engineering, electrical engineering, and computer science. PDEU is known for its state-of-the-art infrastructure, dedicated faculty, and a strong emphasis on hands-on learning and industry collaborations. PDEU stands out for its multidisciplinary approach to education, integrating technical expertise with a broader understanding of global challenges. The campus fosters an environment of intellectual curiosity, encouraging students to think beyond conventional boundaries. PDEU's commitment to excellence is reflected in its rankings, accreditations, and partnerships with leading academic and industrial organizations worldwide.



Schools and Departments

  • School of Technology (SoT)

    Offers programs in Civil, Mechanical, ICT, and Computer Engineering, focusing on practical learning and industry collaboration. Students benefit from cutting-edge laboratories, interdisciplinary projects, and guidance from experienced faculty. SoT also emphasizes research-driven education, preparing students to tackle real-world engineering challenges.

  • School of Energy Technology (SoET)

    Specializes in Chemical, Electrical, and Petroleum Engineering with emphasis on sustainable energy solutions. SoET is known for its groundbreaking research in renewable energy, carbon capture, and energy efficiency technologies. The school actively collaborates with industry leaders to offer internships and research opportunities.

  • School of Management (SoM)

    Provides MBA programs in Energy, Infrastructure, and General Management, integrating business acumen with energy expertise. The curriculum is designed to develop leadership skills, strategic thinking, and an understanding of the energy market dynamics, equipping students for successful careers in business and management.

  • School of Liberal Studies (SLS)

    Offers diverse undergraduate and postgraduate programs fostering creativity, critical thinking, and interdisciplinary learning. SLS encourages students to explore various fields, including economics, psychology, international relations, and the arts, promoting a well-rounded educational experience.

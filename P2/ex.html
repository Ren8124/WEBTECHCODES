Pandit Deendayal Energy University (PDEU)

Pandit Deendayal Energy University (PDEU), located in Gandhinagar, Gujarat, India, is a premier institution offering a wide range of programs in energy, technology, management, and liberal studies. With a focus on excellence in education and research, PDEU prepares students to meet global challenges with innovation and sustainability.

Schools and Departments

  • School of Technology (SoT)

    Offers programs in Civil, Mechanical, ICT, and Computer Engineering, focusing on practical learning and industry collaboration.

  • School of Energy Technology (SoET)

    Specializes in Chemical, Electrical, and Petroleum Engineering with emphasis on sustainable energy solutions.

  • School of Management (SoM)

    Provides MBA programs in Energy, Infrastructure, and General Management, integrating business acumen with energy expertise.

  • School of Liberal Studies (SLS)

    Offers diverse undergraduate and postgraduate programs fostering creativity, critical thinking, and interdisciplinary learning.
